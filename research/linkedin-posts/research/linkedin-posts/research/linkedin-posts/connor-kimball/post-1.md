# Post by Connor Kimball
**Date:** March 18, 2026
**URL:** https://cairrot.com/blog/aeo-reporting-guide-marketing-agencies/ (cross-posted content, shared on LinkedIn)

**Summary (paraphrased):**
Connor Kimball argued that traditional SEO "ranking position" has effectively been a dead metric for years, and that AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is simply forcing the wider market to catch up to what advanced SEOs already knew. His core point: in AI search, there are no fixed positions 1 through 10 — a brand might appear in 6 out of 10 AI-generated answers today and 3 out of 10 tomorrow, because every query generates a fresh response. He recommends agencies shift client reporting away from rank tracking and toward "visibility" metrics — a combination of mention rate, share of voice, and citation frequency — combined with actual downstream traffic and conversion data. He notes manual spreadsheet tracking breaks down past 1-2 clients, which is why dedicated AEO tracking tools became necessary.

---
**Why this post matters:** Kimball runs an AEO analytics platform used by real paying clients across SaaS, industrial, legal, and fintech sectors, so this isn't theoretical — it reflects what he's seeing agencies actually need to report to clients in practice, and reflects a broader shift in what "success" even means in an AI search context.
